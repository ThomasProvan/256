$$ \pi_{(i,j)}\lambda_{(i,j)} = \pi_{(i,j-1)}\lambda_{(i,j-1)}p_{(i,j-1)(i,j)} + 
                                \pi_{(i,j+1)}\lambda_{(i,j+1)}p_{(i,j+1)(i,j)}

$$ \pi_{(i,0)}\lambda_{(i,0)} = \pi_{(i+1,0)}\lambda_{(i+1,0)}p_{(i+1,0)(i,0)} +
